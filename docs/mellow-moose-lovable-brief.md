# Mellow Moose Burgers Design Brief for Lovable

Use this as a first-pass visual design brief. Do not include private strategy notes, personal details, or anything not intended for a third-party design tool.

## Project Goal

Create a mobile-first custom menu landing page for Mellow Moose Burgers, a playful smash burger food truck in Siloam Springs, Arkansas.

This should feel like a real customer-facing menu page, not a generic SaaS template, not a Clover clone, and not a basic Facebook/Google listing.

## Business Details

- Business name: Mellow Moose Burgers
- Business type: Smash burger food truck
- Location: Griffin's Food Court, 825 S Mt Olive, Siloam Springs, AR 72761
- Primary order link: Clover ordering page
- Primary customer action: Order on Clover
- Secondary customer actions: Get Directions, Call, Share Menu
- Brand status: Formerly Dos Gordos Tacos, now rebranded as Mellow Moose Burgers

## Brand Feel

The page should feel:

- Warm
- Playful
- Mellow
- Bold
- Local
- Burger-focused
- Easy to scan on a phone

Avoid:

- Corporate SaaS styling
- Cold tech dashboard styling
- Fake testimonials
- Stock-photo feeling
- Text over food photos
- Generic restaurant template energy
- Overly polished fine-dining design

## Visual Direction

Use a bright, energetic food truck personality:

- Bright orange from the truck/brand
- Cream or warm off-white background
- Dark brown or nearly black text
- Teal or sage accent
- Yellow/gold accent for badges or highlights
- Rounded but not childish
- Crisp menu cards
- Strong hierarchy for item names and prices

The visual tone should feel like a fun local burger truck with real personality, not a franchise.

## Page Structure

Create these sections:

1. Hero
   - Logo-first or brand-first
   - Business name
   - Short description
   - Clear buttons: Order on Clover, Get Directions
   - Hours/location visible near the top

2. Local Favorites
   - Best sellers and owner picks first
   - Large food image areas
   - Item name, short description, price, and badge
   - Badges like Local Favorite, Best Seller, Mellow Pick

3. Full Menu
   - Grouped by category
   - Easy to scan
   - Price clearly visible
   - Item notes visible without clutter

4. Hours and Location
   - Clear daily/hour blocks
   - Location and directions button
   - Food truck context

5. QR / Share Area
   - A section that makes sense for a QR code on the truck, counter, social profile, or printed material

6. Popup Menu Mode
   - Include a design concept for a banner or toggle where Dos Gordos popup menu can take over when activated
   - This should feel like a special event mode, not a separate unrelated site

## Menu Content Guidance

Use real menu-style content only. If exact menu data is missing, create structure without pretending final prices/items are verified.

Suggested items to include as design examples:

- The OG Smashburger
- Blazing Moose Fries
- Hearty Shroom and Cheese Burger
- Torta Burger
- Jalapeno Ranch Bacon Burger
- Cowboy Burger
- Slammer Jammer

Use simple menu wording:

- Item name
- Price
- 1 short description
- Optional badge

Do not write long marketing paragraphs for each food item.

## Image Guidance

Use crisp image containers and assume real food photos will be supplied.

Important:

- Do not place text over food photos.
- Make image areas feel intentional even if a photo is missing.
- Use a logo/truck/brand image area in the hero.
- Use consistent food image ratios in menu cards.

## Copy Tone

Friendly, direct, local, and useful.

Examples:

- Smashed fresh burgers, loaded fries, and rotating specials in Siloam Springs.
- Start with what people come back for.
- Local favorites up front, full menu below.
- Find the truck, order ahead, or share the menu.

Avoid:

- "Revolutionize your dining experience"
- "AI-powered transformation"
- "Unlock growth"
- "World-class culinary journey"
- Any income or ranking guarantees

## Functional Requirements

The page should include:

- Mobile-first layout
- Desktop layout
- Order on Clover button
- Get Directions button
- Call button
- Share menu / QR section
- Local Favorites section
- Full menu section
- Popup menu banner/toggle concept for Dos Gordos mode

## Output Request for Lovable

Build a polished first-pass responsive web page design. Focus on visual quality, menu readability, and brand personality. This is a design prototype only. Do not add fake testimonials, fake awards, fake social proof, or unsupported claims.
